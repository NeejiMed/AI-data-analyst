# Business Context & Domain Knowledge

## Company Overview
We operate a B2B SaaS analytics platform serving enterprise, SMB,
and consumer segments across four regions: North, South, East, West.

## Revenue Model
- Subscription software licenses (Analytics Pro, Analytics Basic, Data Pipeline Suite)
- Infrastructure services (Cloud Storage, API Gateway, ML Model Hosting)
- Professional services (Consulting, Training, Support contracts)
- Primary revenue driver: Software subscriptions at 60% of total revenue

## Key Business Definitions

### Revenue
Total revenue includes all completed orders. Refunded orders are excluded
from revenue calculations. Revenue is recognized at order completion date.

### Churn
A customer is considered churned if they have had no completed orders
in the past 90 days and their account is marked inactive.
Churn rate = churned customers / total customers at period start.

### Customer Segments
- Enterprise: Annual contract value > $10,000, dedicated support
- SMB: Annual contract value $1,000–$10,000, standard support
- Consumer: Annual contract value < $1,000, self-service

## Seasonality Patterns
- Q4 (Oct–Dec): Strongest quarter due to year-end budget spending,
  typically 40–60% above quarterly average
- Q2 (Apr–Jun): Weakest quarter, typically 20–30% below average,
  caused by budget freeze periods after Q1 renewals
- Q1 and Q3: Moderate, near-average performance

## KPI Targets
- Gross Margin Target: > 75% (current: 81.81% — above target)
- Refund Rate Target: < 15% (current: 20.58% — above target, needs attention)
- Average Order Value Target: > $1,500
- Customer Retention Target: > 85% annually
